Privacy policy of JobTimes


1.Information collected: The privacy policy should outline what types of personal information the website collects from users, such as name, email address, phone number, and employment history.

2.Use of information: The policy should explain how the website uses the information collected, such as to match job seekers with potential employers or to send email newsletters about job openings.

3.Data security: The policy should describe how the website secures user data and protects against unauthorized access, such as through encryption and firewalls.

4.Data sharing: The policy should disclose if the website shares user data with third parties, such as potential employers or advertising partners, and under what circumstances.

5.Cookies: The policy should explain if the website uses cookies or similar tracking technologies, and how they are used.
